## Alfie's Visual Design

Alfie is a vintage-style cartoon character inspired by Alfred Adler. The art style is mid-century modern illustration with a warm, retro feel.

### Character Features
- **Older gentleman** — bald on top with white/silver hair on the sides, swept back
- **Round face** — warm peach/orange skin tone, big friendly grin showing teeth
- **Round glasses** — thick dark green outlines, circular lenses, iconic feature
- **Small mustache** — subtle, sits above the grin
- **Bow tie** — always orange/coral, signature accessory
- **Suit jacket** — sage/olive green, sometimes with a white shirt collar visible
- **Small leaf/sprig** — appears near the collar or lapel as a nature accent
- **Proportions** — slightly chibi/cartoon, big head on a smaller body
- **Expression** — always joyful, warm, mischievous, approachable

### Art Style
- **Retro screen-print / risograph aesthetic** — visible paper texture/grain throughout
- **Bold outlines** — thick dark forest green linework
- **Limited color palette** — strictly 4 colors:
  - **Peach/warm orange** (#E8A95B-ish) — skin, bow tie, accents
  - **Forest green/dark green** (#2D4A3E-ish) — outlines, dark elements, backgrounds
  - **Sage/olive green** (#8BA67A-ish) — suit, secondary elements
  - **Cream/warm white** (#F5E6C8-ish) — backgrounds, highlights
- **No gradients** — flat color fills with texture overlay
- **Halftone-like grain** — speckled texture simulating vintage printing
- **Thick black (dark green) outlines** — everything has bold stroke weight
- **Circular badge format** — icons often use a circle crop with dark green border

### Poses & Variants in the Reference Art
- **Juggling** — tossing objects playfully (magic/multitasking)
- **Puppets** — holding hand puppets (playful helper)
- **Top hat & magic wand** — spinning hat, pulling rabbits (magical helper)
- **Magic hat** — reaching into a top hat with sparkles (making things happen)
- **At desk** — sitting at a wooden desk, looking out window at a community scene (thoughtful, watching over)
- **Cool guy in mirror** — wearing sunglasses, looking at reflection with regular glasses (self-awareness, humor)
- **Running through maze** — navigating a maze with icons (gears, calendars, documents) around him (workflow/productivity)
- **Tongue out badge** — playful logo with a tiny Alfie standing on his own tongue (signature/mascot)
- **Holding wrench + pencil** — tools of building and creating (skills)
- **Holding gear** — workflow/systems (operations)
- **Eyes closed, content smile** — peaceful, leaf accent (coaching/mindfulness)

### Image Generation Prompt Template

When generating Alfie images, use this as a base prompt and adapt:

```
A vintage retro cartoon illustration of "Alfie" — a cheerful bald older gentleman with round glasses, an orange bow tie, and a sage green suit jacket. Mid-century modern screen-print style with visible paper grain texture. Limited 4-color palette: peach/warm orange skin, forest green outlines, sage green suit, cream background. Bold thick outlines, no gradients, flat color fills with halftone texture. [SCENE/ACTION DESCRIPTION]. Warm, joyful, mischievous expression. Small leaf sprig near collar.
```
