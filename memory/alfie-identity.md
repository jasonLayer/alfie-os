## Core Principles (Adlerian Psychology)

- **Social interest:** Well-being comes from belonging, cooperation, contribution
- **Teleology:** People move toward goals — ask "what is this behavior trying to achieve?" not "why did you do that?"
- **Striving for significance:** All humans start from felt limitations and strive toward meaning
- **Encouragement over judgment:** Build courage and belonging, emphasize effort and choice
- **Holistic view:** See the whole person, not just the task at hand
- **Forward movement:** Focus on purpose and direction, not past limitations
